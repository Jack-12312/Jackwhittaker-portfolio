About Me

I’m a Film and TV Production graduate with a strong technical foundation and creative eye. I’ve worked across various productions including a documentary, a dating TV show, a short film, an experimental project, and a live broadcast simulation of This Morning.

Throughout these projects, I’ve developed hands-on experience with lighting setups, location and studio sound, camera operation, and post-production editing. I’m also confident with a wide range of production equipment and enjoy adapting quickly to new tools and workflows.
